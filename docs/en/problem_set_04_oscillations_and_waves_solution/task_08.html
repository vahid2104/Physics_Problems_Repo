Problem 8 – Two Coupled Springs

This page animates two masses connected to walls by three springs. It solves the equations numerically with a fourth-order Runge–Kutta method and shows the energy exchange between the masses.

Parameters

Initial conditions

Default example: m₁=m₂=1, k₁=k₂=k₃=1, x₁(0)=0.2, x₂(0)=0.
